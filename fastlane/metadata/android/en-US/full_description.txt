<a href="https://baraud.io/">BarAudio</a> provides audible realtime stock notifications on US equities. Stock price and volume are updated once per minute via a short, customizable audible notifications which can be customized by language, voice and frequency.

Never miss that ideal entry or exit trade because you were away from the stock chart. Move the chart from your screen to your ears and spend more time on your life.

Features:

• Unlimited stock alerts
• Realtime data feed
• Price and volume indicators
• All US equities
• Customizable alerts and frequency
• Custom watch lists
• Simple and intuitive UI
• Sign in with Google or Facebook
